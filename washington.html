Washington

The end of the summer in 2018 saw my first visit to the Pacific Northwest in Washington state. I spent the first week with dad, exploring the Olympic Coast at Shi Shi Beach as well as parts of the Mt. Baker Wilderness. The next two weeks were spent with two friends from school. The three of us backpacked the Tatoosh Ridge overlooking Mt. Rainier NP, the Alpine Lakes Wilderness (near the middle of Section J of the PCT), and the famed Enchantments Wilderness near Leavenworth.
