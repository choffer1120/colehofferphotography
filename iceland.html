Iceland

Four friends and I travelled to Iceland for two weeks in August 2017, spending our time circumventing the island on the famed Ring Road, as well as exploring the wonders of the interior Highlands. Our travels included the 4-day Laugavegur Trek, a hut-to-hut backpacking trail from Landmannanaular to Thorsmork, as well as the Fimmvörðuháls Pass from Skogafoss to Thorsmork. We also visited many other beautiful spots, not limited to waterfalls like Haifoss, Kvernufoss and Bruarfoss, as well as adventures like summitting Kirkjufell and watching the Northern Lights in Thorsmork.
